⚠️ Safety Tips for Diwali

✅ DO's

  • Always buy green crackers (environment friendly).
  • Light crackers in an open area.
  • Keep a bucket of water or sand nearby.
  • Maintain a safe distance while lighting crackers.
  • Wear cotton clothes while bursting crackers.

❌ DON'Ts

  • Do not light crackers in hand.
  • Do not wear synthetic or loose clothes.
  • Never try to re-ignite a failed cracker.
  • Do not use glass bottles or containers to launch rockets.
  • Avoid bursting crackers near hospitals or schools.
